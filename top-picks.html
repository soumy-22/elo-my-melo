WHY I RECOMMEND THESE PRODUCTS

I am calling this a product recommendation page because I care about products that offer great value for money. I want people to find the best possible option for their use case scenarios. I recommend products that I personally reviewed and tested. Some of these recommended products aren't reviewed by me. But using my experience, research, exper­tise and by comparing them with my reviews, I can assure you that these tech products can be declared as pound for pound champion or budget king.

Portable Speakers
The JBL Charge 5 has deep punchy bass with nice mids and highs. With great design, decent overall sound and premium build quality, I think the charge 5 justifies its price. The Soundcore Motion+ is a pound for pound champion in terms of how much quality you can get within the price range. It has great bass and mids with excellent highs that provide nice details and sound­stage. It's very powerful and the soundcore app with real custom eq is a huge deal. I love my motion+ so much.

The Soundcore Boost Gen 2 is a budget king because of its excellent all-around sound. This small portable speaker has very impressive powerful bass for its size. Around the 50 dollar mark the soundcore boost is the champion. Last but not least the Tribit Stormbox Blast is a jbl boombox killer. It can give you a performance close to the boombox 2 within a way way low price. Overall quality of the stormbox blast is also amazing.

TWS Earbuds
The Mpow M30 TWS earbuds are also true budget king. It has a great all-round sound with a ton of high-end features. The M30 buds offer insane value for your money. Apple AirPods Pro is a premium category earbud that has the best transparency mode in any in-ear type earbud. The ANC is also great and the sound signature is nicely balanced. The airpods pro justifies its high-end price. Another exceptional product is the Earfun Free Pro 2 tuned by Oluv. He really knows how a balanced tuning should sound like.

He tried to minimize peaks and dips in the frequency response to get a natural real life like sound. The Free Pro 2 as a budget device with his tuning can offer you a reference grade earbud. In the earbuds category last but not least the Pixel Buds A-series look gorgeous. Their simple clean low profile design is just phenomenal. They offer overall a balanced decent quality sound under budget price. With some budget oriented useful features, these Google buds are very special on their own.

Smartphones
Most people will agree with me that the Google Pixel 5a is the best budget smartphone that money can buy. Pixel 5a with Google's stock Android, software support, great hard­ware and exceptional computational photography, sets its place to whole another level in the budget category. The OnePlus Nord 2 is a great all-round smartphone. It has excellent performance, clean software, great ultrawide and main camera, comfortable design and budget price tag. The Nord 2 is a very good upgrade from the original Nord.

The Nothing Phone 1 is here to make a change in the smartphone market because of its special design. Even though it's a great all-rounder, the design and aesthetics are the main attraction of this phone from the company Nothing. The Samsung Galaxy A52s is a great phone to recommend. It has great One UI skin on top of Android, good performance, top tier image processing and quality build that you can rely on. Finally but importantly the iPhone 13 is a flagship category smartphone with one of the best cameras, the fastest chip, premium build and design, high-end features and crazy smooth software. This is the most consistent and reliable flagship phone.

I highly recommend these products. Sometimes I cannot rank them under any common key characters because while most of them are all-rounder, some devices actually offer more in certain areas. A lot of other experienced reviewers have also recommended them highly. I am very passionate about my work and I hope you will get enough information from my website. At last I want to say that choose something that you think you can love, enjoy and be happy with. Good luck everyone ...
