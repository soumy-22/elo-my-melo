ABOUT THE WEBSITE
Elo My Melo



From my passion and love for technology, I built this website to share my opinion with the world. My website is unique and special in its own way. You will get a clean, refined and modern user experience while visiting this website. Articles and reviews represent my research data and honest thoughts about the topics, which hold high standards in my opinion. Now I will tell you about making the website.

I am studying Computer Science and Engineering. At some point there was a web development course that I had to pass. From then I started designing a website and I loved the whole web designing process. Since I started my youtube channel I always wanted to make my own website for written contents but I wasn’t sure about making it by myself. I actually think most websites look very common, traditional, messy and uneven. Because of that I always wanted to create my own unique, clean and modern design that will look different from most websites.

My target was to make a simple and clean website that will look elegant, attractive and modern. Making a fancy website wasn’t my goal. I tried to make it look professional and gorgeous. So when I found that I actually love designing, I started working hard to make my imagination real. I designed it using my imagination thinking how a modern simply gorgeous website should look. It’s totally my own unique design and it’s nothing like any other traditional website. Every section in my website is clear and clean. The navigation I created on my website is very unique, easy, simple and clean to offer you a better user experience. Article pages are mobile responsive.

I did put a lot of effort while designing it and I have added a lot of small details to make it simply attractive. I have also used a lot of cool colors across my website to give it nice accents. I’m continuously working on my site to make it more refined, polished and clean because I care about a good user experience and user interface. The website’s design is already refined and polished enough.

I post tech articles like news, product reviews, test results, my honest opinions and so on. All contents and pages are categorized for the visitors to find articles easily. Top navigation menus and submenus will help you to find what you want quickly. You can submit feedback using the comment section. Please support the website to grow big. Take care of yourself and good luck everybody.
